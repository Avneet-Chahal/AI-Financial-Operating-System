# AI-FOS — User Stories

> As a [person] / I want to [action] / so I can [outcome]

1. **As** Priya, a salaried professional juggling 5 different finance apps, **I want to** see a single dashboard summarizing my spending, investments, and loans, **so I can** understand my financial health without switching apps.

2. **As** Priya, **I want to** get a plain-language explanation of where I overspent this month, **so I can** adjust my habits without digging through transactions myself.

3. **As** Priya, who has an active home loan, **I want to** get one actionable recommendation per session, **so I can** take a concrete next step instead of just viewing numbers.
