# AI-FOS — Product Spec

| # | Element | Details |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Who** | Priya, 27, a salaried professional juggling 5 finance apps with no single clear view of her money. |
| 2 | **Core Flow** | Opens one dashboard → AI-FOS pulls her data → gives a plain-language summary → she acts on one recommendation. |
| 3 | **Must-Have (v1)** | Unified dashboard, expense tracking, plain-language summary, one actionable recommendation, secure login. |
| 4 | **Nice-to-Have (Later)** | Fraud detection, tax/loan/goal agents, voice assistant, mobile app. |
| 5 | **Not This** | Not a full feature list, not an architecture document. |
